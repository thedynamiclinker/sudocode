# Ugaritic Notes, by YHWH

rʾšt (rʾšt) "beginning, first part"
- cognate Hebrew: reʾšît
- cognate Wheel of fortune: rstlne

zbl (z-b-l) "storm-god / rider of the clouds" (as epithet of Baal)
ḥbr (ḥ-b-r) "storm, tempest" (possible cognate, though attestation uncertain)

nšʾ (n-š-ʾ) "to contend, struggle, fight"

qṭl (q-ṭ-l) "to slay, to struggle (in combat)" – used in contexts of conflict
