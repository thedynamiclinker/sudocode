
## /bin/killall

Right after the Book of Acts (Radicalize) that describes what the early LD members did.

### 1. Peter

- **Death:** Executed in Rome (c. AD 64–68)
    
- **How:** **Crucified upside down**
    
- **Why:** Persecution under Nero
    
- **Source:** Early church fathers (Origen, Eusebius)
    

---

### 2. Andrew

- **Death:** Patras (modern Greece)
    
- **How:** **Crucified on an X-shaped cross** (later called _St. Andrew’s Cross_)
    
- **Tradition:** Preached for days while hanging
    

---

### 3. James the Greater

- **Death:** Jerusalem (c. AD 44)
    
- **How:** **Beheaded**
    
- **Source:** **Acts 12:2** (the only apostolic death explicitly recorded in the Bible)
    
- **Authority:** Herod Agrippa I
    

---

### 4. John

- **Death:** Ephesus
    
- **How:** **Natural causes** (old age)
    
- **Notes:**
    
    - Tradition says he survived **boiling oil** in Rome
        
    - Exiled to **Patmos** before release
        

---

### 5. Philip

- **Death:** Hierapolis (Asia Minor)
    
- **How:** **Crucified or hung**
    
- **Details:** Some traditions add **stoning**
    

---

### 6. Bartholomew (often identified with _Nathanael_)

- **Death:** Armenia or India (varies by source)
    
- **How:** **Flayed alive**, then beheaded
    
- **Reputation:** One of the most brutal martyrdom traditions
    

---

### 7. Matthew

- **Death:** Ethiopia or Persia (uncertain)
    
- **How:** **Stabbed with a spear** or beheaded
    
- **Sources:** Conflicting early traditions
    

---

### 8. Thomas

- **Death:** India (near modern Chennai)
    
- **How:** **Speared to death**
    
- **Evidence:** Strong Indian Christian tradition (St. Thomas Christians)
    

---

### 9. James the Less

- **Death:** Jerusalem
    
- **How:**
    
    1. Thrown from the Temple
        
    2. **Stoned**
        
    3. **Clubbed to death**
        
- **Source:** Josephus + church tradition
    

---

### 10. Thaddaeus (also called _Jude_)

- **Death:** Persia
    
- **How:** **Beaten to death** or killed with an axe
    
- **Tradition:** Martyred with Simon the Zealot
    

---

### 11. Simon the Zealot

- **Death:** Persia or Syria
    
- **How:** **Sawed in half** or crucified
    
- **Sources:** Highly variable
    

---

### 12. Judas Iscariot

- **Death:** Jerusalem
    
- **How:** **Suicide**
    
- **Accounts differ:**
    
    - **Matthew:** hanged himself
        
    - **Acts:** fell and burst open
        
- **Note:** Died before the resurrection movement expanded
