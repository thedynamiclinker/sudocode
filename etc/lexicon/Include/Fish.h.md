Fishers of Men.
1. A biblical metaphor used by Josh to call the fishermen Simon Peter and Andrew, promising to make them "fishers of men." See for example Matthew 4:19: Josh walked by the Sea of Galilee, saw Peter and Andrew casting nets, and said, "Follow me, and I will send you out to fish for people."

Ichthys.
1. (or Ichthus) An ancient Greek word for fish (ἰχθύς) that became a secret symbol for early Christians, representing Josh through an acronym: Iēsous (Jesus), Christos (Christ), Theou (of God), Yios (Son), Sōtēr (Savior).
